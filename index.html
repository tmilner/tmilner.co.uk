About Me

Hello! I'm Tom and I'm a Staff Applications Engineer at Medidata.

There I helped create Site Payments, the payments solution for Medidata's clinical trial platform, and I am now shaping the way the company adopts GraphQL.

I mainly work in Scala, writing services that deal with everything from CRUD operations to complex event processing and rule engines. While working at Medidata I have developed a keen interest in DDD, clean architecture and event driven systems which I believe has lead to a well designed code base with clear seperation of business rules and technical concerns.

Before Medidate I worked at IBM, where I worked with Java and C to develop the clustered IO Caching and RAID controller layers of Spectrum Virtualize, which is a storage virtualisation product available across a range of IBM hardware.
